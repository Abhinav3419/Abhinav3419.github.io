Background & Rationale

CVD claims 17.9 million lives annually (WHO). In India ‚Äî home to 77 million+ diabetic adults ‚Äî coronary artery disease strikes nearly a decade earlier than in Western populations, driven by premature metabolic syndrome, genetic predisposition, and rapidly urbanising lifestyles.

All established ML models ‚Äî Cleveland, Framingham ‚Äî were built on Western Caucasian cohorts. Their applicability to Indian clinical practice is fundamentally limited. This project fills that critical gap: the first cardiac risk ML model grounded in authentic South Asian clinical ground truth.

Research Objectives
‚Ä∫ Develop ML cardiac risk model surpassing Western-trained benchmark accuracy for Indian patients
‚Ä∫ Exhaustive feature analysis ‚Äî all 13 individual, 78 pairs, 286 triplets, and higher-order combinations
‚Ä∫ Validate against real Indian hospital outcomes under authentic clinical practice conditions
‚Ä∫ Publish in peer-reviewed medical or biomedical engineering journal as original scientific contribution
‚Ä∫ Assess commercial viability as clinical decision-support tool ‚Äî with full Digital Data Protection Law compliance

Research Methodology ‚Äî Five Phases
01
Individual Feature Analysis
13 Features
Each of the 13 clinical parameters ‚Äî demographics, haemodynamic, biochemistry, ECG indices, and advanced imaging ‚Äî is individually correlated with heart disease probability, with full pathophysiological justification grounded in standard cardiology literature (Braunwald, Levine, Mattu & Brady, Agarwal, Klabunde).
02
Pairwise Combination Analysis
78 Pairs
All 78 clinically meaningful feature pairs systematically evaluated for synergistic predictive value, categorised into six clinical domains (age-based, symptomatic, vascular, ECG, cardiac-functional, and metabolic), and ranked on a five-star combined correlation scale.
03
Triple Combination Analysis
286 Triplets
Priority triplets assessed for emergent synergistic ‚Äî not merely additive ‚Äî predictive power. Highest-rated triplets span multiple diagnostic modalities. The triplet cp + exang + oldpeak constitutes a triple-positive stress test with near-diagnostic certainty for haemodynamically significant CAD.
04
SHAP-Guided Algorithmic Discovery
Higher-Order
Beyond triplets, SHAP values and Random Forest feature importance scores guide higher-order combination discovery algorithmically ‚Äî replacing manual selection bias with statistically rigorous, reproducible feature engineering consistent with current best practices in medical AI.
05
Clinical Validation Against Indian Outcomes
Real-World
All five ML models evaluated against real Indian hospital outcomes. Primary metric: AUC-ROC. Clinically weighted Recall (sensitivity) is prioritised ‚Äî false negatives in cardiac diagnosis carry disproportionately high clinical cost. Every iteration documented with full transparency.
